ABOUT US

ARON BIKES is a retail and bike service shop established in 2014, with a second branch opened in 2019. It is owned by Ronnie Valenzuela Rosaceña and Arlyn Bandoy Rosaceña. Customers can CONTACT the store.


Customers are families, students, and bike professionals aged 3 years old and above from Antipolo City and nearby areas. The business provides affordable bikes, repairs, and cycling equipment. Challenges include low online visibility, manual inquiries, and strong competition. A website can help increase visibility, allow online inquiries, and improve marketing and sales.


The business sells bicycles, bike parts, and accessories, and offers repair and maintenance services. It currently operates as a physical store with plans to expand online. Main customers include students, families, and bike professionals. The business wants more customers, stronger online presence, easier inquiries and bookings, and online selling. They want a website to promote products and improve customer communication.


ARON BIKES is a local bike shop that has been serving the community for over a decade. We are passionate about cycling and dedicated to providing high-quality bikes, parts, and accessories to our customers. Our mission is to promote a healthy and active lifestyle through cycling while offering exceptional customer service.
